Welcome to the Business Administration Department

As new technological advances and an increasingly global economy transform the way businesses operate, it is extremely important for any nation to train the men and women who can lead their economies through this change. Ashesi seeks to train such people for Ghana by providing a well-rounded business education that emphasizes the fundamentals of business, and introduces students to the latest trends that are changing the business world.

Welcome to the Computer Science Department

The computer science department is housed in the Ashesi Engineering Building. While focused on similar values, most Computer Science courses occur within the building. Ashesi's Computer Science programme focuses not only on software design and programming, but also on empowering young people to be at the forefront of African growth by harnessing the power of technology. Students in the Computer Science programme are taken through robust foundational courses that prepare them to engage new technologies, and create software tools for African needs.

Welcome to the Engineering Department

The engineering department is housed in the Ashesi Engineering Building. Most engineering classes occur within labs that look like the header image, and are typically covered with engineering equipment. The Engineering courses are partitioned into the following categories: General Engineering Courses, Computer Engineering Courses, Electrical and Electronic Engineering Courses, and Mechanical Engineering Courses. The course challenges students to analyze the design and function of systems by using principles from different engineering fields including computer, electrical and electronic, and mechanical engineering.
